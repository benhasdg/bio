Optimizing Data Analytics for Institutional Growth

At the University of Alabama at Birmingham, managing digital platforms for four distinct departments requires more than just maintenance—it requires insight.

By architecting custom data lifecycles with Google Tag Manager and Looker Studio, we've transformed raw service requests and traffic patterns into a roadmap for strategic growth. This approach ensures that every pixel serves a purpose and every update is backed by data.
